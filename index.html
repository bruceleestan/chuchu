about me

Hello! My name is Ryan Taizo Chu.

I am a visualization artist based in Rancho Cucamonga, California.
I have four years of experience in CG as a 3D generalist, also coming from a traditional 2D background. My skills have been honed through many projects, collaborating with supervisors as well as peers in different disciplines in the pipeline.

Growing up loving martial arts movies, I also fell in love with the movement of the human body—and of the camera that follows them.
I love being able to tell my own stories through the medium of 3D and I hope I am able to inspire others to do the same!
